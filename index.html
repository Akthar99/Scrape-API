About

Imagine you have a tool that can fetch information from Wikipedia. Let's say you're interested in learning about JavaScript. With this tool, you can ask for information about JavaScript and specify how much information you want. Once you've made your request, the tool will gather data from Wikipedia and provide you with a response. The response will contain all the links related to the topic you asked about, such as different pages on Wikipedia that mention JavaScript. This way, you can easily explore more about the topic by following those links.
